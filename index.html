Research interests

  • Discrete geometry

      and its link to continuous elasticity
    • Compatibility conditions of polynomial systems
    • Delaunay/Voronoi tessellations in all its variants: weighted (regular), double, ...
  • Equilibrium thermodynamics
    • How to measure thermodynamic quantities in hard-sphere systems
  • Small-Reynolds-number Hydrodynamics
    • Hydrodynamic influence of walls on particles/droplets/bubbles
    • Diffusion of Brownian particles in driven fluidic systems
